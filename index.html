Services

Workers' Compensation Evaluations

The Hamlin Psyche Center is a psychiatric practice with over 30 years experience in psychiatric evaluation and medical-legal reporting and over 25 years experience in evaluating and treating injured workers.

Disability Evaluations

The Hamlin Psyche Center also provides evaluations for our patients with Social Security and Long and Short-Term Disability claims on an as-needed basis.

Psychotherapy and Psychiatric Management

Our treatment professionals specialize in providing psychotherapy and psychiatric medication managmenet services for industrial injuries.
